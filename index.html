About

I am grad student in the MIT Media Lab's Lifelong Kindergarten Group and an alum of Olin College of Engineering. Previously, I worked part-time at MIT Lincoln Labs in the Human Language Technology Group and was a Technical Associate at Startup Institute Boston. Before moving to Boston, I had lived my entire life in Reston, Virginia, one of the first socially-engineered towns in the US, and spent (almost) all my time on the soccer field.

I am interested in using natural language processing and computer science to build educational experiences that help people learn about themselves, their communities, and the world around them.
